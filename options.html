How to find your Notebook ID:

  1. Open your preferred notebook in NotebookLLM.
  2. Look at the URL: https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/YOUR_ID_IS_HERE
  3. Copy that ID and paste it above.

If left empty, the extension will open the main NotebookLLM page, and you will have to select a notebook manually.
